Designed to give civil servants the data they need to transform government

Cross-Government Service Data is built for civil servants who work with multiple organisations across government.

It provides authoritative high-level metrics along with additional context to give them a rounded view of each department, agency and service.

It can be explored through the Cross-Government Service Data website or exported as a CSV.

What data is available

Where appropriate data is also aggregated to the agency, department and UK government level.

The purpose of the service

  • How it works
  • Who the typical users are
  • How frequently it's used
  • How long it takes for a transaction to end in an outcome

The number of transactions received by the service

  • Online
  • Phone
  • Paper
  • Face-to-face

The number of transactions ending in an outcome

  • Ending in the user's intended outcome (where available)

The total number of calls to the service

  • The percentage that are to get information
  • The percentage that are to chase progress
  • The percentage that are to challenge a decision

How the data was collected

  • Department and agency responsible for it
  • When it was last updated
