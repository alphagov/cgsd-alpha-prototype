Get data about government services

This tool gives government the data to make better decisions about services. It helps civil servants to design government to better meet user needs.

View all or find something specific

How it works

You can use this tool to:

  • see what’s happening with government services in order to prioritise work
  • find best practice so you can introduce standards
  • demonstrate the value of your strategies

What data is available

Aggregate and trend data about:

  • transaction and call volumes
  • channels used to access services
  • reasons users call about services
  • reasons services contact users

This data is for all government services, not just digital ones.
