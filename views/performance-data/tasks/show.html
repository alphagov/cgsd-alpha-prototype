About this task

What user need does the service meet?

The majority of users are young people leaving school or college and planning to go to university.

Who are the typical users?

To get a loan to support their tuition and living costs for the duration of their studies.

How often do people use the service?

As many times as they go to university â€” for most people, no more than once in a lifetime.

How does the service work?

All applications, whether online or paper, are exported to PDF. They are then sent to be reviewed by a decision maker. The decision maker assesses the applicant's suitability for a student loan, and grants it accordingly. Their decision is then entered into the system and an automated response is sent via post.

How long does it take users to complete a transaction?

An applicant will receive a result within 2 weeks.
